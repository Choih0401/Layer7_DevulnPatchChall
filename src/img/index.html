how to

Fill your forms at the registration page to get started. After that, check the outputs that you're supposed to make by writing codes. Then wear a blindfold, and select the programming language that you want to use. Now you're ready to go for the challenges. You can continue to the next level if the output that you've made is correct. Cheer up!
